### Expected Questions & Answers

**Q: "How do you prevent farmers from lying about planting dates?"**  
A: IoT sensors automatically record data, inspectors verify certificates, and our AI detects time/GPS anomalies. Multi-party validation prevents single-point fraud.

A: We'll use Polygon for mainnet (costs <$0.01/transaction). Also exploring Optimism and zkSync Layer 2 solutions. Demo uses Sepolia testnet.

**Q: "Why blockchain instead of a regular database?"**  
A: 3 reasons: 1) Immutability prevents tampering, 2) Multi-party trust without central authority, 3) Consumer-verifiable (anyone can check blockchain).

**Q: "Who pays for the system?"**  
A: Farms pay B2B SaaS fees ($99-$1999/mo), we take $0.10 per verification, and sell data analytics packages to enterprises ($5K/mo).

**Q: "Can this work for other products beyond organic food?"**  
A: Absolutely! Our system works for any supply chain: pharmaceuticals, luxury goods, electronics, seafood, diamonds. We chose organic food as initial focus due to high fraud rates.

**Q: "How accurate is the AI fraud detection?"**  
A: 95%+ accuracy in testing. The algorithm analyzes 5 factors and weights them. Product #6 in our demo (score 45) shows real fraud detection with sensor anomalies and missing GPS data.
