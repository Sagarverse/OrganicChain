### Before You Present:
- [ ] Test live demo 3 times
- [ ] Have backup screenshots ready
- [ ] Time yourself (aim for 6:30 to allow Q&A)
- [ ] Practice transitions between slides
- [ ] Memorize key numbers (30%, $500M, 3 seconds)

### During Presentation:
- **Energy**: High enthusiasm, show passion for the problem
- **Eye Contact**: Look at judges, not screen
- **Pacing**: 30 seconds per slide (except demo slides)
- **Body Language**: Open gestures, confidence
- **Voice**: Clear, varied tone (not monotone)

### Demo Best Practices:
- **Always have fallback**: If MetaMask fails, show video recording
- **Explain as you go**: Don't let silence hang while transactions confirm
- **Highlight UI**: Point to glassmorphism effects, animations
- **Show fraud detection**: Product #6 with low score is your secret weapon

### Q&A Preparation:
Expect these questions:

4. **"Revenue model?"**
   - Answer: B2B SaaS ($99-$1999/mo), transaction fees ($0.10/verification), enterprise licensing
